What is Cyber Security?

Cyber security is the practice of protecting digital systems, networks, and sensitive data from cyber threats. It involves implementing security measures to defend against attacks, unauthorized access, and data breaches.

Why is Cyber Security Important?

With the increasing reliance on technology, protecting digital assets is crucial. Cyber security safeguards personal and business information, ensures data privacy, and prevents financial losses caused by cyber crimes.

Common Cyber Threats

  • Phishing Attacks: Fraudulent attempts to steal sensitive information via fake emails or messages.
  • Malware: Malicious software such as viruses, worms, and trojans designed to harm devices.
  • Ransomware: A type of malware that locks or encrypts data and demands payment for its release.
  • Denial-of-Service (DoS) Attacks: Overloading a system to make it inaccessible to users.
  • Data Breaches: Unauthorized access to confidential information leading to data leaks.

Cyber Security Solutions

To protect against cyber threats, individuals and organizations should implement strong security measures such as firewalls, antivirus software, multi-factor authentication, and regular software updates.
